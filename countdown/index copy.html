PAYDAY 3 will release on the 21st of September, 2023.

System Requirements
Minimum:
  • Processor: Intel Core i5-9400F
  • Memory: 16 GB RAM
  • Graphics: Nvidia GTX 1650 (4GB)
Recommended:
  • Processor: Intel Core i7-9700K
  • Memory: 16 GB RAM
  • Graphics: Nvidia GTX 1080 (8GB)
Development Engine

The game is developed on Unreal Engine 4. The developers are planning on upgrading to Unreal Engine 5 post launch.

Multiplatform

The game will be available on PC, PlayStation 5 and Xbox Series X/S. Unreal Engine motor ensure that the PC and console versions are the same game.

Crossplay

PAYDAY 3 supports crossplay with all supported platforms, which means that you can play with your friends no matter where they prefer to play. You will also be able to avail of cross-progression, so you can heist from your preferred platform at any time.
